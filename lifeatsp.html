My First Impression of SP

I was browsing through SP's website when I saw the webinar for International Students. I signed up for the webinar as I believed that it was my opportunity to learn more about SP and the courses offered. Even though I have always wanted to study at SP, after the webinar, I was certain that SP is my dream polytechnic!

With my passion in programming, I was certain that SoC is my dream school. I looked through the different courses and I came across Information Technology. Everything (the modules and electives, and even the career options) seemed perfect! I put DIT as my first choice of course and now I am glad that I made the right decision.

When I browsed through the Life@SP page, I was intrigued by the over 100 CCAs. With my passion in IT and Videography, I was certain that I would join SPIC and SPVC. Since I also wanted a Sports CCA, bowling was one of my choices. Even though I also had a keen interest in guitars, I knew too many CCAs would be burdensome.

Common ICT Programme

DCITP will help you lay a strong foundation in programming and IT fundamentals. At the end of the year 1 semester 1, you will be able make an informed choice in selecting one of the three IT courses that you wish to pursue:

  • Diploma in Information Technology (DIT)
  • Diploma in Infocomm Security Management (DISM)
  • Diploma in Applied AI & Analytics (DAAA)
Diploma in
Information Technology

In Singapore, there continues to be a strong demand for IT professionals. In Diploma in Information Technology (DIT) course, students get to do real customer projects! DIT focuses on Software & Applications development with three specialist tracks:

  • Software Development
  • User Experience (UX)
  • Immersive Simulation

Diploma in
Infocomm Security Management

Being a Diploma in Infocomm Security Management (DISM) student gives you the competitive edge for a boost in your future studies and career, with an interesting curriculum that covers the:

  • Security technology
  • Security management
  • Information technology
  • Law
  • Teamwork & communications
Diploma in
Applied AI and Analytics

Interested in developing the next AI application that benefits our world? If yes, the Diploma in Applied AI & Analytics (DAAA) is perfect for you! AI & data analytics are the key technologies for transforming every industry, including healthcare!
